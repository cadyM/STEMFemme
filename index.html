Stem Femme

Mentoring Girls in STEM

Stem Femme aims to support and empower girls and nonbinary youth who have a passion for science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) fields. By connecting these young people with positive female and nonbinary role models in the industry, Stem Femme hopes to help them develop the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to succeed in these fields.

Let's feminize STEM

Women only represnet 30% of the STEM industry. by providing young girls and nonbinary youth with mentors, we can help to support the next generation of scientists and engineers, build a more inclusive and diverse industry, and pave the way for a brighter future for all.

Empowerment · Confidence · Support · Growth

Mentorship can help you gain so many valuables skills that can help you with your goals in the STEM industry. In addition to technical skills in the feilds, metors can help empower and support you and help you not feel alone in a vey male dominated industry
