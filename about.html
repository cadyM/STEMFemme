Stem Femme

Mentoring Girls in STEM

STEM Femme is an organization that seeks to inspire and empower girls and non-binary youth in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM). The organization provides opportunities for these young people to connect with positive female and non-binary role models in STEM fields, who can offer guidance, advice, and mentorship.

The goal of STEM Femme is to help these young people develop the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to pursue careers in STEM and become leaders in their fields. The organization provides mentors that can help build their skills and connect with like-minded individuals.

Through its programs and initiatives, STEM Femme aims to create a supportive and inclusive community for girls and non-binary youth interested in STEM. By providing role models and resources, the organization hopes to encourage more young people to pursue STEM careers and help close the gender gap in these fields.
